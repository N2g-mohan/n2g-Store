Return Policy

At N2G Store, we want customers to receive correct and quality products. If you receive a damaged, wrong or defective product, you may request a return.

Return Eligibility

  • Return request should be made within 7 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused and in original condition.
  • Original packaging, invoice and accessories should be available.
  • Damaged or wrong product must be reported with clear photos.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Used, damaged by customer or missing-packaging products.
  • Personal care, medicine or hygiene-related items after opening.
  • Products marked as “No Return” on the product page.

Refund Process

After the returned product is received and checked, refund will be processed to the original payment method or store wallet.

Return approval depends on product condition and seller verification.

Shipping Info

N2G Store tries to deliver products safely and quickly to your address. Delivery time may depend on product availability, location and courier service.

Delivery Time

  • Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 7 working days.
  • Remote areas may take extra time.
  • Delivery time may increase during festivals, sales or bad weather.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charge may depend on product weight, seller and location.
  • Some products may be eligible for free delivery.
  • Final shipping charge will be shown during checkout.

Order Tracking
